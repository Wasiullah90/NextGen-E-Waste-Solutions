What We Do

Our Services

End-to-end e-waste management for businesses of all sizes across Pune and Maharashtra.

🏭

PCB & Manufacturing Scrap

We collect PCB offcuts, rejected boards, SMT line waste, solder dross, and fabrication rejects directly from your production floor.

💻

IT Asset Collection

Bulk pickup of retired laptops, desktops, servers, networking equipment, and peripherals from IT companies and BPOs.

⚙️

Machinery & Equipment

End-of-life industrial machines, PCB manufacturing equipment, and electromechanical systems collected and responsibly dismantled.

📦

Component & Dead Stock

Expired or damaged electronic components, dead stock inventories, and component reels from dealers and distributors.

📄

Compliance Documentation

Every pickup comes with a Certificate of Recycling and documentation required under E-Waste Management Rules 2022.

🔄

Scheduled Collections

Set up a recurring monthly or quarterly pickup schedule — we handle the logistics so your team never has to think about it.
