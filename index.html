Who We Are

Born from a simple question:

"Where does your old phone really go?"

In 2018, our founder Priya Sharma saw e-waste being dumped illegally on the outskirts of Mumbai. Shocked by the toxic pollution it caused, she left her IT job and started NextGen E-Waste Solutions from a tiny warehouse in Andheri.

Today we operate across Mumbai and Pune with a team of 120+ certified technicians, a state-of-the-art processing facility, and partnerships with over 800 businesses and government departments.

Our mission is simple: zero e-waste in landfill by 2030.

The Process

Your Device's Journey ♻️

Here's exactly what happens, step by step.

Step 1

📝 You Book Online or Call Us

Fill in our quick form — tell us what devices you have and choose a pickup date. Takes less than 2 minutes! You'll get a confirmation email with your booking reference.

Step 2

🚚 Our Team Comes to You

A trained NextGen technician arrives at your address at the scheduled time. They'll inventory every device, label it with a unique serial number, and load it into our secure, GPS-tracked vehicle.

Step 3

🔒 Data is Destroyed First

Before any device is dismantled, we wipe all storage media following NIST 800-88 standards. Hard drives are either securely wiped (3-pass overwrite) or physically shredded. No data can ever be recovered.

Step 4

🔧 Devices Are Sorted & Assessed

Devices that can still work are tested and refurbished for reuse. This extends their life! Devices that can't be repaired are carefully dismantled into categories: plastics, metals, glass, circuit boards, batteries.

Step 5

⚗️ Materials Are Recovered

Circuit boards go through our urban mining process — we extract gold, silver, copper, and rare earth metals. Plastics are pelletised and sold to manufacturers. Metals are melted and reused. Nothing goes to landfill!

Step 6

🏅 You Get Your Certificate

Within 5 business days, you receive your Certificate of Recycling with details of every device recycled, your carbon offset, and full compliance documentation for your records.
